1. What is collected

This site collects nothing about you. No accounts, no emails, no cookies for tracking. Your calculator inputs stay on your device.

The only data stored is in your own browser's localStorage: the subjects and marks you enter (so the priority list can read them), and your theme preference (light or dark). Both are readable only by you, on your device, and clearing your browser data wipes them.

2. What is NOT collected
  • No name, email, or login
  • No IP addresses stored by this site
  • No analytics trackers, no pixels
  • No third-party cookies
  • No advertising networks

3. Hosting

The site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server logs (request URL, timestamp, user agent) as part of running a web server. These logs are handled by Vercel under their own privacy policy and are not accessed or used by this site.

clause 3
04
4. Shared links

The calculator has a share button that creates a URL with your inputs encoded in the query string. This URL is generated in your browser and copied to your clipboard. The site itself does not store, log, or see the contents of these shared links.

If you share the link with someone, anyone with the link can see the inputs that produced those results. Treat it like sharing a screenshot.

clause 4

5. Third parties

Fonts are loaded from Google Fonts. Google may log a font request when your browser fetches the stylesheet. This is governed by Google's privacy policy, not this site's.
